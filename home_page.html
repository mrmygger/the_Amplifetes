The Band

The Amplifetes is a 4-piece band from Sweden. Influences range along a broad spectrum, including 60s psychedelia, the Electric Light Orchestra, The Ramones, Elvis Costello, David Bowie, and Chicago Trax Records. The individual members have had success as songwriters and producers, working with artists such as Kelis, Britney Spears, Madonna, Kylie Minogue, and Grandmaster Flash.

Peter Aagren

Peter Aagren is a Swedish songwriter, producer, musician and artist. Credits include The Amplifetes, Tove Styrke and Peter, Bjorn & John. He started his career as singer for Swedish powerpop band Grass Show. He went on to sign with renowned publishing company Murlyn Music as a songwriter. In 2008, he joined Swedish electro pop project The Amplifetes, together with Henrik Jonback, Henrik Korpi and Tommy Spaanheden.

Tommy Spaanheden

born April 20, 1970 in Gothenburg , is a Swedish music producer working in the house , electronica , breakbeat genre under several aliases. He has participated in various groundbreaking electronic acts, including Cataclysm , Inquisagon , Incosm and Psychogenetic Soundforce .
